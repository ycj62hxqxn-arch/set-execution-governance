What is RAM?

RAM (Regulatory Architecture Model) is BPB Solutions’ structured framework for governance, compliance, and operational discipline. It integrates technical controls (compliance automation, audit protocols, authority logic) and operational processes (review cycles, onboarding, risk oversight) to ensure capital integrity and regulatory alignment across all business lines.
